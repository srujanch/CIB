    2011 - Acquisition finance, Corporate finance advisory

    Standard Bank full-service investment banking team has successfully partnered with Eqstra Holdings Ltd in a R5.15 billion recapitalization transaction, comprising a R650 million rights issue and a R4.5 billion debt restructuring. The transaction leaves Eqstra with a revitalized balance sheet and re-positions the company for growth.

  • Integri-T Property Fund ZAR 285 million, medium term loan, South Africa

    2011 - Property finance

    Standard Bank Provided finance to the Integri-T Property Fund totaling R284 million over a portfolio of 26 properties consisting of retail and commercial buildings predominately located in the Western Cape. The funding is for a five year term. The intention is to grow the fund by acquiring additional quality commercial property.

  • Standard Bank acts as co-debt underwriter and funder for ZAR9.2 billion capital raised in a Brait led transaction, South Africa

    2011 - Acquisition finance, Corporate finance advisory

    Standard Bank acted as co-debt underwriter and funder in a Brait led transaction which was transformational for the private equity industry. The deal allowed Brait, listed on the JSE, to evolve from a third party fund manager to an investment holding company with interests in Pepkor and Premier Foods.

  • Standard Bank acted as the South African Investment Banking advisor in Steinhoff's EUR 1.2bn acquisition of Conforama SA,

    2011 - Corporate finance advisory, Debt capital markets

    Standard Bank acted as the South African Investment Banking advisor for Steinhoff's acquisition of Conforama SA. We were also the underwriter and funder of the related ZAR300 billion perpetual preference shares and co-manager on their two offshore convertible bonds that raised a further EUR857 million.

  • Toyota Financial Services ZAR760 million bond issue, South Africa

  • Stockbell BFN (Pty) Ltd ZAR 71 million development loan, South Africa

    2011 - Property finance

    Standard Bank provided development finance totaling R71 million to Stockbell BFN (Pty) Ltd for the development of 170 luxury student accommodation sectional title units for phase 1 of the development to be known as Unilofts BFN (Pty) Ltd in Bloemfontein. The property is in close proximity to the University of Free State.

  • Growthpoint ZAR 2 billion medium term loan, South Africa

    2011 - Property finance

    Standard Bank co-funded Growthpoint's 50% acquisition of the V&A Waterfront in March 2011. The V&A Waterfront is a mixed-use property development around the historic Victoria and Alfred Basins which formed Cape Town's original harbor. The V&A is South Africa's most visited destination attracting up to 21 million visits annually and is a unique landmark South African asset.

  • Atterbury Investment Holdings ZAR 1.6 billion, short term funding, South Africa

    2011 - Property finance

    Standard Bank has provided an 18 month term loan facility of R1.6bn to Atterbury Investment Holdings (Pty) Ltd to fund their working capital requirements, secured by quality assets. The debt facility materially increases SBSA's overall debt position to the Atterbury Group and should result in mutually beneficial ancillary business going forward.
