Pricing

At Standard FX Trader, we believe we offer you a favourable rate of return, by trading on our most popular currency pairs on spreads, as low as, 0.7 pips. Our transparent and competitive pricing structure is backed by our commitment to deliver the best possible execution on every trade.

Standard FX Traders pricing is market driven. A proprietary rate engine aggregates prices from the platforms institutional trading partners and publishes real-time quotes to our clients. Since these quotes are derived directly from interbank prices, our dealing spreads reflect the available forex market liquidity and provides highly competitive pricing in all market conditions.

  • Market-Driven Pricing: Our quotes are derived directly from the prices we receive from our access to deep interbank liquidity, so our dealing spreads always reflect available liquidity in the foreign exchange market
  • Low Minimum Spread: When market liquidity is high, we pass this on to you in the form of our tightest possible spreads – the minimum spread.
  • Capped Max Spread: As liquidity changes, our spreads will adjust accordingly, but only up to a maximum spread of 3 times the typical spread in any currency pair. This cap protects you against excessively wide spreads during major economic announcements and other volatile market conditions, representing 99.99% of all price quotes during normal market hours.
  • Precise Quotes: Our real-time executable prices are quoted in more precise 0.1 pip increments – this extra digit of precision is designed to deliver tighter, more accurate bid/ask spreads
  • Price Improvement Ability: If you submit a market order and a better price is available at the time your deal is executed we'll give it to you, up to a 2 pip improvement .

You pay no fees and no commissions to trade on Standard FX Trader. Your only effective cost is the dealing spread - the difference between the bid price and the ask price. (There may be typical transactional fees and charges levied to you by your bank when opening and FCA or Offshore Banking account, for converting and transferring funds into your Standard FX Trader Account).

Spot metals

Spot gold and silver give you added flexibility on Standard FX Trader. There are no commissions to pay trading spot metals, since your only cost is included in the bid/offer spread. Additionally, you also enjoy low 1% margin
requirements*, competitive dealing spreads and small contract sizes.

An added advantage of trading in spot gold and silver is that spreads are quoted in terms of pips, where each pip is worth 10 cents.
