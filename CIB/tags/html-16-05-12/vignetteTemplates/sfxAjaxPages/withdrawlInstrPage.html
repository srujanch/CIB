Withdrawal Instructions

Funds can be withdrawn from Standard FX Trader quickly and with minimum hassle. All withdrawal requests are processed within two business days of receipt.

To access a withdrawal form, please login to MyAccount and click on the “Withdrawals “ link on the right hand side of the MyAccount Home page. Please complete and submit the form.

Depending on the funding currency of your account, you will incur a USD 20 / EUR 20 / GBP 20 withdrawal fee.
