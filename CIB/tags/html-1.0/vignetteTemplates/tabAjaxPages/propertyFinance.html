Property finance

Property is one of the largest and most widely held asset classes globally. Property assets tend to be held for a variety of reasons and by a broad range of owners and investors.

Standard Bank provides financing solutions that aim to optimise the value in individual properties or across portfolios, recognise the long term nature of the asset and meet the specific needs of our clients who develop or own property

Why talk to us?

  • Our experts understand the general segments of the property market (retail, commercial, industrial, residential development) as well as more specialised property classes including healthcare facilities, hotels and resorts. We partner with clients to develop the most appropriate solutions for property development as well as term funding requirements.
  • We are able to offer financing solutions for individual properties, property portfolios and listed property funds.
  • Our property team is part of Corporate & Investment Banking's investment banking team, enabling seamless access to other specialist financing areas to deliver the most appropriate financing solution for our clients.
  • One of our specialist focus areas is to deliver financing solutions to corporate clients who have significant property holdings. Our objective is to work with clients to unlock optimal value in their property portfolio.
  • We offer property financing solutions across Africa, combining the skills of our in-country teams with the expertise of our specialist property finance team based in Johannesburg, South Africa.
