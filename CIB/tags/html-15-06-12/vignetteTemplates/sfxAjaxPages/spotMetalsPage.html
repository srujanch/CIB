• What are the symbols for spot gold and silver?

    The symbol for spot gold is XAU/USD and silver is XAG/USD.

  • What are the trading hours for trading spot gold and silver?

    Spot gold and silver trading is available from Sunday 24h00 (CAT) to 23h00 (CAT) on Friday during US summers and Monday 01h00 CAT to Friday 24h00 (CAT during US winters. Be advised of the potential for illiquid market conditions outside of New York trading hours - 08h00 to 17h00 (ET) - (or 14h00 to 23h00 (CAT) during US summers and 15h00 to 24h00 (CAT) during US winters). Spot gold and silver trading also follows the Chicago Mercantile Exchange (CME) holiday closures.

  • What is the maximum number of contracts I can trade with one click?

    Each price quote is valid for trades of up to 25 contracts.

  • Is there a limit to the amount of gold or silver I can hold in my account?

    The maximum position a client may hold at any given time is 1000 oz for spot gold and 50,000 oz for spot silver.

  • How is margin calculated for spot gold and silver?

    The normal margin on spot metals is 100:1, or a 1% margin factor. Remember that higher leverage magnifies both gains and losses.

  • What is one point worth when trading spot gold and silver?

    One pip of spot gold is 10 cents or US$0.10 per lot (10 oz). One pip of spot silver is worth US$5 per lot (500 oz).

  • What is the spread when trading spot gold and silver?

    The typical spread you will see for spot gold is 55 pips. For example, 900.25/80. Spot silver has a typical spread of 4. Spreads will vary depending on market liquidity.

  • Can I hold positions over the weekend and major holidays?

    Yes.

  • Is there an expiry date on spot gold and silver contracts?

    There is no expiry date when trading spot gold and silver. As long as you maintain the required margin, your position will remain open until you choose to close it. As with forex positions, open positions on gold and silver automatically roll forward to the next day's value date following the close of New York trading at 17h00 (ET) or 23h00 (CAT) during US summers and 24h00 (CAT) during US winters.

  • What is one lot of spot gold and spot silver equal to?

    One lot or one contract of spot gold is equal to 10 troy ounces. One contract of spot silver is equal to 500 troy ounces.
