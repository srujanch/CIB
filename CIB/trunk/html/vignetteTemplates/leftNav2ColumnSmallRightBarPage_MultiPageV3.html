Overview

Raising capital to finance business expansion or working capital requires financing solutions considerate of a business's specific circumstances and needs. Term loans can provide the ideal vehicle for providing such debt funding, as they can be customised to each client's unique requirements while at the same time offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness.

Why talk to us?

  • Our specialist team combines relevant sector expertise with a detailed understanding of your business, collaborating with other specialist units in the bank to provide the most appropriate debt solution to meet your funding objectives.
  • Standard Bank's focus on emerging markets globally, and our presence in key markets around the world, enables us to offer competitive cross-border solutions in various currencies.
  • Our distribution channels, both locally and through our London-based syndication desk, means we are well placed as both a funding arranger and as a participant in broader bank syndication offerings.

Products and services

Lending facilities with tenors longer than three months are structured to meet our clients' specific funding requirements, and to address issues relevant to a particular counterparty. Although each structured facility is different, some of the more common variations include:

  • Specific project funding for working capital / bridging purposes
  • Revolving or fixed drawdown and repayment arrangements
  • Bullet or amortising repayment profiles
  • Secured or unsecured funding
  • Variable or fixed interest rates
